Academic Information

Academic Pathways

King’s College offers two main pathways: NCEA and Cambridge (CIE).

NCEA Pathway

Flexible assessment with internal and external exams, leading to University Entrance.

Cambridge (CIE) Pathway

Exam-based courses (IGCSE, AS, A Level) recognised by universities worldwide.

Subjects Offered

Students can study a variety of junior and senior subjects:

  • Junior: English, Maths, Science, Social Studies, Languages, Arts, Technology
  • Senior: Literature, Calculus, Physics, Chemistry, Economics, History, Music, Digital Technologies

Academic Support

Tutors and guidance are available to help students succeed.
